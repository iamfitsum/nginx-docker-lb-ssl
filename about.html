About Us

MasterBirana is the leading provider of courses, currently available in more than 7 languages all over the world. Our courses have helped many people for over 10 years.

What is MASTERBIRANA?
 MASTERBIRANA is a website, with tutorials and references on any thing you want to master in, covering most aspects.

The site derives its name from the english word MASTER which means to get the knowlegde and skill that allows you to do , use, or understand very well and BIRANA which are the ancient books of Ethiopia made with animals skin that are written about Astrology, Medicine, Mathimatics and many other Knowledges that are still mystical.

MASTERBIRANA was originally created in 2020, by Birana INC., an Ethiopian software development and consulting company
